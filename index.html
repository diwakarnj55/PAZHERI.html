Envisaging Rapid Growth
And Wider Presence


Pazheri is a fast emerging Indian conglomerate that wide interests in followings verticals: Real Estate and Property Development, Gold Jewellery Retail, Steel Manufacturing, Rubber Processing, Automobile Sales and Allied Businesses. Today Pazheri is on the path of strong growth with fresh investments in key sectors and envisaging rapid growth through wider presence across a spectrum of India’s sunshine industries with the aim of becoming a top ranking corporate by 2025.

Based in Mannarkkad, in Palghat District, Kerala, the Group has played a significant role in the industrial and business environment of the State with its multifaceted businesses and multi-dimensional manufacturing enterprises. The Group’s pioneering spirit has been witnessed over three generations who have led the group to take up new businesses in tune with the needs of the times. The Pazheri Group is thus playing a major role in building enterprise across Northern Kerala and becoming a significant contributor to the socio-economic development of Kerala State in general. The Pazheri Group is harnessing its assets and furthering its businesses with the aim of leading in its chosen areas and providing the best in services and manufactured products to customers in India and across the world.

The group has major presence in sectors like:

Real Estate and Property Development
Steel Manufacturing
Jewellery Retail, Distribution and Manufacturing
Event Management
Rubber and Dealership
Agro Farming
Rubber Processing
Automobile Sales
Allied Services

Glorious Legacy Of Pazheri

The passion of the Pazheri Group of Companies is to create, produce and deliver the ultimate in customer experience. This desire to offer the best that can be takes its birth long back in history, centuries ago. The promoters of the Group trace their legacy to Yemen where their forefathers were involved in the spices trade with India and primarily the port cities of Kerala like Muzuris and Calicut.
